The Opportunity

A New Asset Class at the Intersection of Space, Energy, and Bitcoin

Intercosmic Energy holds a patent-pending platform that converts orbital solar energy directly into Bitcoin — creating a lossless digital value pipeline from space to Earth with no ground-based transmission infrastructure required.

The Timing

Three Converging Trends Make This the Right Moment

  • Declining launch costs: Falcon 9 rideshare has pushed per-kilogram costs below $5,000. Starship is projected to reduce this below $100/kg at scale — making large constellation deployment economically feasible for the first time.
  • Megaconstellation buildout: Over 1.1 million satellites are planned for deployment across Starlink, Starcloud, Qianfan, Guowang, Amazon Leo, and OneWeb. Each is a potential platform for our technology — without requiring additional launches.
  • Bitcoin's price trajectory: Based on a 26.4% CAGR projection, Bitcoin's value per mined coin is expected to increase significantly through 2030 and beyond, compounding the revenue potential of every satellite deployed.

The window to establish patent primacy and secure early licensing relationships is open now. As megaconstellation operators finalize satellite architectures, integration becomes significantly more complex — and expensive — after the fact.
